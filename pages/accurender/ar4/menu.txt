** Displaying the AccuRender Pulldown Menu in [[rhino:autocad|AutoCAD]] 2009 and 2010 **

The [[rhino:autocad|AutoCAD]] 2009 and 2009 default workspaces with the Ribbon Bars do not show the AccuRender menu.

Either from the Workspace toolbar or in the status bar and on the Workspace pane (looks like a gear),

select "[[rhino:autocad|AutoCAD]] Classic" as the workspace.

Now the AccuRender menu will be visible.
