===== Fowler Middle School =====

This is an in-depth, hands-on, three-day workshop taught by teachers for teachers. With all the Rhino educational resources you will discover like the Rhino Curriculum Guide and ideas for class projects, you will have students working in 3-D on the first day of the term.

The shop room has 30 pc computers with Rhino 6 installed. Teachers will have access to lasers with cardboard and .125 and .25 in wood. 3-D printers and vinyl cutters are also available.
