Digital Sketching with PanelingTools for Rhino and Grasshopper

Rajaa Issa, chief architect of Rhino's PanelingTools plug-in shows how to use PanelingTools to develop pattern concepts. She also demonstrates the basics of using PanelingTools for parametric modeling with Grasshopper.

{{vimeo>41953491}}

----
This is the full presentation given by Rajaa Issa during the [[http://www.rhinofablab.com/photo/albums/ufablab-at-the-university-of?xg_pw=&id=4439072:Album:16101&commentPage=&page=2|D-O-F (Design+Optimization+Fabrication) workshop at the University of Florida]] on February 11, 2011.

The presentation is broken into three parts. The first shows surface rationalization and unrolling.  The second explores cladding and the third shows a weaving example.  You can also [[http://download.mcneel.com/s3/mcneel/misc/DOF_UF2011.zip|download example files]] used in the presentation (3DM Files, 24MB).
