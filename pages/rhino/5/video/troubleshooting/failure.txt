======Rhino Video Troubleshooting======

**To determine which video card <color red>Rhino</color> sees in your computer:**
  - Start <color red>**Rhino**</color>.
  - On the **Tools** menu, click **Options**.
  - Find and expand the **View** tab (located near the bottom of the list on the left), and then click **OpenGL**.
  - Under **Video Hardware & Driver Information**, note:
      - The **video card manufacturer**, the first line.
      - The **video card adapter**, second line.
      - The **driver date**. You will need this information in future steps.
