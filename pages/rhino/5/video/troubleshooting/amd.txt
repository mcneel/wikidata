====== Video Troubleshooting: AMD ======

To update your AMD video driver:

  - Close Rhino 
  - Please [[http://support.amd.com/en-us/download|update your video driver]]
  - Choose the graphics card make and model as well as the Windows operating system you're using if it isn't automatically detected by AMD's site
  - Download the suggested driver from AMD
  - Run the installer you downloaded to update your graphics card driver
  - Restart your computer if prompted to do so
  - Open Rhino to test if your issue has been fixed
