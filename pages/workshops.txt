====== Teacher Workshops 2020 ======

[[https://wiki.mcneel.com/workshops/fowler|Fowler Middle School Workshop Details]]

===== Kulshan Middle School =====
This is an in-depth hands-on four-day workshop. Rhino educational resources like the Curriculum Guide and ideas for class projects. With all the resources you will be shown and what you've earned, you'll have students working in 3-D on your design exercise within the first week of the term.

Participants will be able to output via vinyl-decal, vinyl-heat press ( bring some shirts!), 3D printers, Laser cutting/engraving, Cnc mill.

===== Banks High School =====
This is an in-depth, hands-on, three-day workshop taught by teachers for teachers. With all the Rhino educational resources you will discover like the Rhino Curriculum Guide and ideas for class projects, you will have students working in 3-D on the first day of the term.

The shop room has 27 Windows computers with Rhino 6 installed. Teachers will have access to For fabrication equipment we have a laser cutter/engraver, CNC plasma, vinyl cutter w/heat press, CNC router, & several 3D printers.
There will be paper, metal, wood, plywood, vinyl, etc. that can be used in fabrication projects. Teachers are welcome to bring their own .125"  and .25" thick wood and plywood, also cardboard and vinyl.

There are a couple of places in Banks for lunch or teachers can bring a sack lunch.

Classroom is in the 'Vocational Building' at the north end of the school. There is a blue door that will be open that faces Main St, where there is some parking, and there is a parking lot that faces the football scoreboard where sliding doors of the shop will be open. Look for signs off Main St.
