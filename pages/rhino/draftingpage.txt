====== Additional Hatch Patterns to Download ======
See more [[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/hatch#more_rhino_hatch_patterns|Custom Hatch Patterns]] for Rhino 8

====Hatch patterns for Rhino 5 and Newer ====

1. Download [[https://wiki.mcneel.com/_media/legacy/en/hatchpatterns_1033.zip|this zip file]] and extract the contents. Rename hatchpatterns.txt to hatchpatterns.pat. 

2. In Rhino 5 or newer, go to //File>Properties>Annotation>Hatch// and simply import hatchpatterns.pat.
