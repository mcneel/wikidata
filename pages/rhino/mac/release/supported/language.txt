====== Does the Rhino for Mac work in languages other than English? ======
> **Summary:** //Which languages does the Rhino for Mac support?//

We currently support Spanish, French, German, Italian, Chinese (Traditional and Simplified), Japanese, and Korean.  Rhino for Mac uses the default language selection built into macOS. 

To use a language that is different from the macOS default, in the application menu:
  - Navigate to **Rhinoceros** > **Preferences...** > **Language**.
  - In the **Language** panel, under **Rhino Language**, select your preferred language.
