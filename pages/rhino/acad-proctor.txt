====Rhino Authorized Academic Proctor====

**Rhino Authorized Academic Proctor**, RAAP is a state certified teacher in an educational institution with valid, official school email and a valid Rhino 5 Educational Lab License.  

The **RAAP** will be allowed to administer the Rhino 5 Proficiency Exams - Academic (Proctored) to students and certify the results and testing environment for compliance with all testing site requirements and procedures.
