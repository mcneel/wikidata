======Video Troubleshooting: What Rhino Sees======

To help diagnose this problem, please determine what brand of video card Rhino sees in your computer. Following the instructions below will also give you the date of your graphics card driver which is a significant factor in solving most display problems:
  - **Start Rhino 5**
  - Run the command **Options** or from the **Tools** menu, click **Options**
  - Expand the **View** tab towards the bottom of the list at the left, then click **OpenGL**
  - In the **Video Hardware & Driver Information** section, read the brand of your video card and note the driver date it is using. 
  - Select your video card manufacturer:
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:intel|Intel]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:nvidia|NVidia]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:amd|AMD]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:microsoft|Microsoft GDI Generic]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:virtual|VMware, Galium, Parallels]]
