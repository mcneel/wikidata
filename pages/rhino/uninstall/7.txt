====== Automating Uninstall of Rhino 7 ======

  -  Download [[https://files.mcneel.com/rhino/8/redist/BurnOut.exe|BurnOut.exe utility]].
  - Run `BurnOut.exe /uninstall f7d93b42-988e-462f-8d0b-4cfe68701f6d`

This will find and uninstall any version of Rhino 7 on the computer. Note that uninstall doesn't remove per-user files that were created during or after installation.
