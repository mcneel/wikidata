====== Problem: Flickering Viewports ======

The symptoms consist of:
  * Flickering viewports when trying to draw or drag geometry around
  * Certain viewports showing up in the wrong place (i.e. the Perspective view shows up where the Top view is and vice versa)
  * Picking not correctly selecting the right piece of geometry
  * Possibly others.
