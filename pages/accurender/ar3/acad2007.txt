** AccuRender 3 and [[rhino:autocad|AutoCAD]] 2007 **
====Question:====
======Does AccuRender 3.1.268 run in AutoCAD 2007.======
=====No. AccuRender 3 wil not be recompiled to run with 2007. The ARX format that will run in 2007 is different from that of AutoCAD 2004/2005/2006.=====
  * AccuRender 4.0 SR2 supports [[rhino:autocad|AutoCAD]] 2007 and [[rhino:autocad|AutoCAD]] 2008, see [[accurender:ar4:autocad2007|AutoCAD2007]]
