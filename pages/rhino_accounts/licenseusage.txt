====== Cloud Zoo License Usage ======

For questions about the CloudZoo license system and [[Team|https://www.rhino3d.com/docs/guides/accounts/#teams]], please see this [[guide\https://www.rhino3d.com/docs/guides/accounts/]].

==== Setup Your Team ====
Go to your Rhino account. Set up a Team, invite users and add your licenses to the Team.


To see what users are using your licenses, pick Cloud Zoo Teams and Licenses
{{:rhino_accounts:cloud_zoo_team_03.jpg?400|}}

Usage Bar will show how many user have a license in use.
{{:rhino_accounts:cloud_zoo_team_04.jpg?400|}}
With License chains of Rhino 6, 7, or 8, checking out one version will show usage for all the licenses in the chain. They are linked or chained together. 

==== Get a report of your license usage: ====
  - Go to your [[Rhino Account|https://www.rhino3d.com/my-account/]]:
  - CloudZoo Teams.
  - Pick on your Team. {{:rhino_accounts:cloud_zoo_team_01.jpg?400|}}
  - And see the usage report on the screen. {{:rhino_accounts:cloud_zoo_team_02.jpg?400|}}

Only the Team owner and admins can see the Rhino usage.
You will need to make a Team member an Admin if you want them to see this.
