======What video manufacturer does Rhino see?=====
//[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/home|Home]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting|Rhino Display Troubleshooting]] 
 > What video manufacturer does Rhino see?//

To determine what video card **Rhino sees** in your computer
  - **Start Rhino**.
  - On the **Tools** menu, click **Options**.
  - Find and expand the **View** tab (located near the bottom of the list on the left), and then click **OpenGL**.
  - Under **Video Hardware & Driver Information**, note the **brand of your video card** and the **driver date**. You will need this information in future steps.

  * Once you have found your manufacturer, **click your video card manufacturer** on the list below:
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:amd|AMD]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:intel|Intel]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:nvidia|NVIDIA]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:microsoft|Microsoft GDI Generic]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:virtual|VMware, Galium, Parallels]]
