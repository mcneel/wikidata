======Rhino for Mac======
[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/mac/home|{{ :rhino:mac:osx.png?128|Mac Rhino Wiki Home}}]]


[[http://www.rhino3d.com/download/wenatchee/5.0/wip|Download]] the most current work-in-progress (WIP) version

Please keep in mind:


  * Rhino for Mac is **under development**:
  * **Still missing** are some important feature, plug-in support, and printing.
  * A new WIP will be available **every week or two** as new features are added.
====System Requirements====
  * 64-bit Core 2 Duo Intel processors or later
  * OS X 10.7 or later.
  * 4 GB RAM (more is recommended)
  * A multiple-button mouse (a scroll wheel is recommended)
  * The [[http://www.3dconnexion.com/products/spacenavigator.html|SpaceNavigator]] is supported.
  * **NOT SUPPORTED**
    * OS X 10.6 or earlier versions
    * The **Spaceball** and **SpaceExplorer**
