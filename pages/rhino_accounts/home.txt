====== Rhino Account License Manager ======

Add your license(s) to your Rhino Account (Cloud Zoo) and use them from any device and/or share them with team members. Login with your [[https://www.rhino3d.com/my-account|Rhino account]] to manage your Rhino licenses.

==== Highlights ====
  * Use your Rhino licenses on Mac or Windows.
  * Use your license in the office, from home, or on the road.
  * Work offline for extended periods.
  * Share your licenses with team members worldwide.
  * Free, with no Windows server to maintain.

==== Requirements ====

  *Requires occasional internet connection: //Rhino occasionally connects to your Rhino account to renew your license. Rhino will remind you to go online well before you are required to do so.//


Rhino must be able to contact:
  * https://accounts.rhino3d.com (TCP port 443)
  * https://api.mcneel.com (TCP port 443)
  * https://cloudzoo.rhino3d.com (TCP port 443)
