=====Video Troubleshooting: What Windows Sees=====
//[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/home|Home]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting|Rhino Troubleshooting]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|Problem: Video driver]] > Video Troubleshooting: What Windows Sees//

  - Determine what video card **Windows sees** in your computer.
    - In **Windows**, click the **Start** button.
    - Type **Computer Management**, and press **Enter**.
    - In the **Computer Management** dialog box, click **Device Manager**.
    - Expand the **Display Adapters** section. You should see a list of display adapters.
   -Select the manufacturer of your display adapter from the list below:
    * [[/rhino/5/video/troubleshooting/intel/amd|AMD]]
    * [[/rhino/5/video/troubleshooting/intel/nvidia|NVIDIA]]
    * [[/rhino/5/video/troubleshooting/intel/intel|Intel]]
