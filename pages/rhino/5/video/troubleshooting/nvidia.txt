====== Video Troubleshooting: NVIDIA ======

To update your NVIDIA driver:

  - Close Rhino
  - Please [[http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us|update your video driver]]
  - Select the graphics card make and model as well as the Windows operating system you're using
  - Download the suggested driver from NVIDIA 
  - Run the installer you downloaded to update your graphics card driver
  - Restart your computer if prompted to do so
  - Open Rhino to test if your issue has been fixed
