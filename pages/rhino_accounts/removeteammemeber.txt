===== Remove Team Members from a Team =====

To remove team members from a team:

  - Login to your [[https://accounts.rhino3d.com/?controller=home|Rhino Account]].
  - Go to the [[https://accounts.rhino3d.com/?controller=groups|Teams]] page.
  - Click on the desired team.
  - Click on the user to remove.
  - Click Remove from Team.
