====== Video Troubleshooting: NVIDIA ======
//[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/home|Home]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting|Rhino Troubleshooting]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|Problem: Video driver]] > Video Troubleshooting: NVIDIA//


Start by updating your video driver.

**To update your NVIDIA driver:**
  - Close Rhino.
  - Click **[[http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us|here]]** to go to the NVIDIA site.
  - Select the **graphics card** make and model and the **Windows operating system** you're using.
  - **Download** the suggested driver **installer**.
  - **Run** the installer.
  - **Restart** your computer if prompted to do so.
  - **Open Rhino** to test if your issue has been fixed.

**Is the problem solved?**
  * **Yes**. YAY!\\
  * **No**. Now what?
