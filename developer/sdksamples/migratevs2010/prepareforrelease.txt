===== Prepare for Public Release - March 29, 2011 =====

When you have a new plug-in that builds properly and installs for the 64-bit version of Rhino built with Visual Studio 2010:

  - Make a version available for download from your web site
  - Link to your download page on the public [[rhino/5/64bitpluginmigration|64-bit migration wiki page]].
  - Tell your users to upgrade after March 29.
