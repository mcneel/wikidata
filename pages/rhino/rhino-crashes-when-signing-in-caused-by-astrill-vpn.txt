====== Rhino crashes when signing in caused by Astrill VPN ======
Rhino crashes when you try to sign in to your Rhino account.

==== Symptoms ====
  - Rhino closes immediately after you click Log in, before the web browser opens.
  - The Rhino crash report dialog appears.\\ The crash report has short call stacks about "ASProxy64".

==== Affect Rhino versions ====
Rhino 6, 7, 8


==== Solutions ====

Uninstall **Astrill VPN**, restart Windows, and try signing in to Rhino again.

If you still need Astrill VPN, try the following steps:
  - Press and hold Ctrl key and click the 3 bars at the top left corner of AstrillVPN client.
  - Click HELP > LSP Uninstall
  - Once done, close Astrill VPN client and restart the computer.
