====== Rhino to Revit ======

**Using IFC to transfer BIM objects to Revit**

====Upgrading Revit's IFC====
Revits default IFC reader is not the best IFC reader available for Revit. This may lead to objects within Revit not importing complete family objects. It is reccomended to download the upgraded reader for Revit 2017 and Revit 2018.  Download the [[https://apps.autodesk.com/RVT/EN/LIST/SEARCH?PRODUCTLINE=RVT&QUERY=IFC&FACET=&COLLECTION=|Revit IFC Plugin here]]
