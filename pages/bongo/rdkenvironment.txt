======Animating RDK Environment======


In Bongo 2.0 you can animate properties in the RDK Environment Editor.

All you need to do is to press the Animate Button on the Timeline and open up the Environment Editor and start to make changes. Bongo adds animation data for every change you make.

Here are to example pictures showing, how you can animate for example the color and the gradient in a 2 Color Gradient Environment.
