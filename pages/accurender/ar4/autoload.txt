====== Preventing AccuRender 4.0 from Autoloading ======

=====How do I disable or prevent AccuRender 4.0 from autoloading at the beginning of each AutoCAD session?=====
  * 1. Download this AccuRender 4 [[http://wiki.mcneel.com/_media/legacy/en/AR4AutoloadManager.zip|Autoload Control Manger]] utility.
  * 2. Unzip and save to the Desktop.
  * 3. Close all instances of [[rhino:autocad|AutoCAD]].
  * 4. Double-click on **AR4AutoloadManager.exe**.
  * 5. Check the Autodesk applications for which you want AccuRender to be loaded.
  * 6. Pick OK.

> **Note:** //Repeat producre to start it loading again.//
