====== Video Troubleshooting: AMD ======
//[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/home|Home]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting|Rhino Troubleshooting]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|Problem: Video driver]] > Video Troubleshooting: AMD//

Start by updating your video driver.

**To update your AMD video driver:**
  - Close Rhino.
    - **Desktop**: Click **[[http://support.amd.com/en-us/download|here]]** to go to the AMD site.
    - **Laptop**: Get updated drivers from the laptop maker's Web site.
  - Select the **graphics card** make and model and the **Windows operating system** you're using.
  - **Download** the suggested driver **installer**.
  - **Run** the installer.
  - **Restart** your computer if prompted to do so.
  - **Open Rhino** to test if your issue has been fixed.

**Is the problem solved?**
  * **Yes**. YAY!\\
  * **No**. [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:NothingWorked|Now what?]]
