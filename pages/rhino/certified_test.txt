=====Rhino Certifications and Authorizations=====

==== Certifications ====
Certification exams are a way to measure knowledge and competency with the 
Rhino 3D software. The exams are offered in a commercial or non-proctored environment or in an academic proctored environment. 

Certifications are simply a way to test the users academic knowledge and competency in the Rhino 3D software and in 3D visualization. Typically proficiency with these Rhino skills allow the user to excel and learn more advanced concepts and continue advancing in their knowledge of 3D visualization.

===Professional===
  * Rhino 5 Level 1 Certified
  * Rhino 5 Level 2 Certified

===Academic===
  * Rhino 5 Level 1 Certified Academic
  * Rhino 5 Level 2 Certified Academic
