====== The Materials Tab ======
The Materials Tab can create a number of different types of materials. The options for a new material are Solid Color, Metal, Glass, Plastic, Glossy, Clear Finish, [[accurender:nxt:documentation:beyond:textures|Textured, Texture Set,]] and [[accurender:nxt:documentation:reference:materials:advanced|Advanced.]] For more information on creating new a new material, click [[accurender:nxt:documentation:basic:materials#Adding Materials to a Drawing|here.]]\\
{{:accurender:nxt:documentation:reference:materials.png|}}\\
It also contains the controls for getting a material from a file, saving a material, [[accurender:nxt:documentation:basic:material_editor|editing a material]], [[accurender:nxt:documentation:basic:materials#Assigning Materials to Objects and Layers|assigning a material to objects and layers]], and mapping materials. It also displays user-created materials and indicates which layer each material occupies.
