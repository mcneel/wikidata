====== The Advanced Material Editor ======
The Advanced Material Editor allows the user to control many specific characteristics of their material. It is organized into four tabs: Main, [[accurender:nxt:documentation:reference:materials:advanced:transparency|Transparency]], [[accurender:nxt:documentation:reference:materials:advanced:texture|Texture Maps]], and [[accurender:nxt:documentation:reference:materials:advanced:bumps|Bumps]]. The Main tab controls color and [[accurender:nxt:documentation:beyond:reflections|reflective finish and highlight.]] There is also a [[accurender:nxt:documentation:reference:materials:advanced:material_tree|material tree]] box that is separate from the tabs.
