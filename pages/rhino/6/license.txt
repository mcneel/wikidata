======Rhino 6 License Options======

> **Summary:** //There are now three Rhino licensing options.//

**[[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/install/wizard/start|Choose the licensing method]]** that works best for you.

There are three ways to use your license [[https://www.rhino3d.com/6/new/licensing-and-administration|Details...]]


We recommend using Cloud Zoo for individuals and Teams for maximum flexibility. We recommend using the LAN Zoo for EDU Lab licenses. A detailed comparison of licensing options can be found at [[https://www.rhino3d.com/6/new/licensing-and-administration|Rhino 6 License and Administration]]

We have changed and clarified the [[https://www.rhino3d.com/6/eula|End User License Agreement]] for Rhino 6.
