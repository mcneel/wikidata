======Rhino with PowerSHAPE======
**PowerSHAPE** is a nurbs-based hybrid surface and solid modeler. It also has the ability to combine triangulated artwork, wrapped onto and trimmed into the model. Delcam call this Total modelling.

http://www.powershape-e.com/general/total_modelling.asp

A completely free version of **PowerSHAPE** (containing the full modelling functionality) is available - called PowerSHAPE-e.

http://www.powershape-e.com
