====== Sectioning Workflows in Rhino ======
by [[rajaa@mcneel.com|Rajaa Issa]], Clipping Section (formerly SectionTools plugin)
For [[https://www.rhino3d.com/8/new/|Rhino 8 or later]].

In this comprehensive video tutorial, learn about Rhino clipping and sectioning workflows using section styles, selective clipping, setting clipped views and extract dynamic vector drawings, then wrap up with Q/A.
[[https://mcneel.s3.amazonaws.com/TrainingDocs/ClippingSectionTutorial/SectioningWorkflowsWeb.html|{{ :training:home:buildingonly.png?700 |}}]]

**Before you start:** 
  - **Download** the model set [[https://mcneel.s3.amazonaws.com/TrainingDocs/ClippingSectionTutorial/Sectioning%20Workflows%20Class.zip|here...]] Unzip into a folder and be ready to open the files.
  - **Open** the handout PDF [[https://mcneel.s3.amazonaws.com/TrainingDocs/ClippingSectionTutorial/Sectioning%20Workflows%20Notes.pdf|here...]]
  - **Start the video tutorial** (~2 hours) [[https://mcneel.s3.amazonaws.com/TrainingDocs/ClippingSectionTutorial/SectioningWorkflowsWeb.html|here...]]
