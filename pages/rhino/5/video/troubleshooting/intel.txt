=====Which video manufacturer does Windows see?=====
//[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/home|Home]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting|Rhino Display Troubleshooting]] > [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|Which video manufacturer does Rhino see?]] > Which video manufacturer does Windows see?//

**To determine which video card <color blue>Windows</color> sees in your computer:**
  - In <color blue>**Windows**</color>, click the **Start** button.
  - Type **Computer Management**, and press **Enter**.
  - In the **Computer Management** dialog box, click **Device Manager**.
  - Expand the **Display Adapters** section. You should see a list of display adapters.



  * Once you have determined the manufacturer, select the manufacturer of your display adapter on the list below:
    * [[/rhino/5/video/troubleshooting/intel/amd|AMD]]
    * [[/rhino/5/video/troubleshooting/intel/nvidia|NVIDIA]]
    * [[/rhino/5/video/troubleshooting/intel/intel|Intel]]
