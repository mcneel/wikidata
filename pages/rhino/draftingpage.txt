====== Additional Hatch Patterns to Download ======

====Hatch patterns for Rhino 5 & Rhino 6====

1. Download [[http://wiki.mcneel.com/_media/legacy/en/hatchpatterns_1033.zip|this zip file]] and extract the contents. Rename hatchpatterns.txt to hatchpatterns.pat. 

2. In Rhino 5, go to //File>Properties>Annotation>Hatch// and simply import hatchpatterns.pat.


===== Hatch patterns for Rhino 4 =====
