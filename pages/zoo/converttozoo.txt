======Convert a Standalone Rhino to Use the Zoo======

> **Product:** //[[zoo:home|Zoo]]//
> **Summary:** //Discusses how to convert a Rhino installation from a standalone license to use the licenses in the Zoo.//

=====Question=====
We have recently installed the **Zoo** and added our licenses to it. How do I **convert** our existing standalone Rhino workstations to use the licenses in the Zoo?


=====Answer=====
To convert a Rhino installation from standalone licensing to use the Zoo, follow these steps:

====Rhino 6 or 7 for Windows ====

  - Run **Rhino 6 or 7 for Windows**.
  - Click the **Tools** menu and then click the **Options** menu item.
  - Click the **Licenses** page.
  - Select the Rhino license and click **Change your license key**.

{{zoo:rhino6win_convert.png?500}}

====Rhino 5 for Windows ====

  - Run **Rhino 5 for Windows**.
  - Click the **Tools** menu and then click the **Options** menu item.
  - Click the **Licenses** page.
  - Select the Rhino license and click **Convert**.

{{zoo:rhino5win_convert.png?500}}

====Rhino for Mac====

  - Run **Rhino 5, 6 or 7 for Mac**.
  - Click the **Rhinoceros** menu and then click the **Preferences...** menu item.
  - Click the **Licenses** option.
  - Select the Rhino license and click **Change your license key**.

{{zoo:rhino5mac_convert.png?500}}
