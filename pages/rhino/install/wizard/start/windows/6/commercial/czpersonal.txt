====== Cloud Zoo - Personal ======

>Note: Use this option to share your single license between all of your computers. Your license will reside on our servers so you can access it from any computer. An initial Internet connection is required. There after, running Rhino with an Internet connection once a week or so will keep your license refreshed. You can run Rhino with no Internet connection for about 2 weeks before it will need to be refreshed.

===Initial Rhino application based setup===
  - Install Rhino or plug-in
  - Start Rhino
    - Follow the defaults
  - Start plug-in
    - Follow the defaults

===Initial Web based setup===
  - **Login to your Rhino Account**
    - Visit https://accounts.rhino3d.com/?controller=home
      - Create a Rhino account if you don't already have one
      - Save your credentials. You will need them later
  - **Add your license to your Personal licenses**
    - On your "My Account" page, click Licenses
    - Under Personal Licenses, click [your name] (me)
    - On the "Licenses [your name] (me)" page, click Add License
    - Enter your license key
    - Click Add License
  - **Start Rhino**
    - Click Login
    - Provide your account credentials
    - Rhino will start

===Other computers===
  - **Install Rhino**
  - **Start Rhino**
    - Click Login
    - Provide your account credentials
    - Rhino will start
