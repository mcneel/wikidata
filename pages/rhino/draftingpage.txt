=====Hatch patterns for Rhino 5=====

1. Download [[http://en.wiki.mcneel.com/content/upload/files/hatchpatterns_1033.zip|this ZIP file]] and extract the contents. Rename hatchpatterns.txt to hatchpatterns.pat. 

2. In Rhino 5, go to File>Properties>Annotation>Hatch and simply import hatchpatterns.pat

=====Create more linetypes and print widths=====

Just edit the text files at:

  * Win XP: C:\Documents and Settings\All Users\Application Data\McNeel\Rhinoceros\4.0\English\Support
  * Vista: C:\ProgramData\McNeel\Rhinoceros\4.0\English\Support
