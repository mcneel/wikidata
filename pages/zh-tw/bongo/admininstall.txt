======於Vista/Win7安裝Bongo 1====== 

**問題:** 當我試著安裝Bongo 1於Vista/Win7系統，顯示 When I try to install Bongo 1 in Vista/Win7, it complains about needing Administrator Rights.

**解決方式:** The installer for Bongo 1 was created for Win2000 and WinXP, prior to the complications of User Account Control in Windows Vista and Win7. To get past this complication:
  - Open the Windows file Explorer and browse to the Bongo CD 
  - Find SetupBongo.exe
  - Right-click it and choose **Run as Administrator**
  - Follow the prompts
