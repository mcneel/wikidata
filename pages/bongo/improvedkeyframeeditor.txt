======New Keyframe Editor======

The Keyframe Editor allows you to edit your keyframes.

You can access the Keyframe Editor by clicking on the icon {{:bongo:keyframeeditor.png|}}, go to the Bongo Menu > Keyframe Editor or type in **BongoKeyframeEditor** on the commandline. You can also access the Keyframe Editor by right clicking on a Keyframe on the Timeline.
