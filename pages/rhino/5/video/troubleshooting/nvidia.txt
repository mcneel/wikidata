====== Video Troubleshooting: NVidia ======
We recommend that you [[http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx?lang=en-us|update your video driver]]

  * After clicking the link above
  * Choose the graphics card make and model as well as the Windows operating system you're using if it isn't automatically detected by Nvidia's site
  * Download the suggested driver from Nvidia 
  * Close Rhino 
  * Run the installer you downloaded to update your graphics card driver
  * Restart your computer if prompted to do so
  * Restart Rhino to test if your issue has been fixed
