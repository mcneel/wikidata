====== Configuring Rhino 5 on Retina MacBook Pro in Windows 7 or Windows 8 ======
To make the best of your super-high resolution Retina MacBook Pro, configure Windows as follows:

  - **Right-click the Desktop** then click **Screen Resolution**
  - At the bottom of the //Change the appearance of your display// page, click **Make text and other items larger or smaller**
  - Select **Medium - 125%**
  - Click **Apply**
  - Close the //Make it easier to see what's on your screen// dialog.
  
This makes all the icons in Windows large enough to see and use.
