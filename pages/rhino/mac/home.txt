======Rhino for Mac======


| [[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/mac/home|{{:rhino:mac:rhino_symbol_mac.png?164|Rhino for Mac Home}}]] | **Rhino 5 for Mac is almost here!** \\ \\ Release Candidate 1 (RC1) is a full commercial product that runs with 90-day evaluation, commercial, and educational (but not WIP) license keys. When you download RC1, you will start your 90-day evaluation period. \\ \\ We will ship RC1 as Rhino 5 for Mac [[http://discourse.mcneel.com/t/reporting-problems-with-mac-rhino/6078|unless you tell us about a significant problem]]. |

**What will happen:**

  - Your Rhino for Mac WIP will stop working on June 3, 2015.
  - Before June 3rd, visit [[http://www.rhino3d.com/download/rhino-for-mac/5/evaluation|http://www.rhino3d.com/download/rhino-for-mac/5/evaluation]] to download RC1.
  - We will send you an email with your Rhino for Mac 90-day evaluation license key and a download link.
  - In the next few weeks, we will announce Rhino for Mac, including special introductory pricing and where to buy.

**Important:**

Some WIP features [[http://discourse.mcneel.com/t/rhino-for-mac-removed-features/19947|have been removed]] from Rhino for Mac.

Rhino for Mac is [[http://www.rhino3d.com/mac-feature-compare/|NOT the same]] as Rhino for Windows.
[[http://discourse.mcneel.com/t/reporting-problems-with-mac-rhino/6078|Please let us know]] if you see a problem that should be fixed before we release.

-----
====System Requirements====

  * Apple Mac with 64-bit Core 2 Duo Intel Processor or later.
  * OS X 10.8.5, 10.9.5, or 10.10 or later.
  * 4GB or more RAM is recommended.
  * NVIDIA or AMD graphics processors are recommended.
  * A multiple-button mouse with scroll wheel is recommended.
  * The [[http://www.3dconnexion.com/products/spacenavigator.html| 3DConnexion SpaceNavigator]] is supported (the driver is required.)
  * [[http://wiki.mcneel.com/zoo/home|The Zoo]] (Only required for network license sharing).
