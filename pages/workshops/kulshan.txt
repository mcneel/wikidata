===== Kulshan Middle School =====
This is an in-depth hands-on four-day workshop. Rhino educational resources like the Curriculum Guide and ideas for class projects. With all the resources you will be shown and what you've earned, you'll have students working in 3-D on your design exercise within the first week of the term.

Participants will be able to output via vinyl-decal, vinyl-heat press ( bring some shirts!), 3D printers, Laser cutting/engraving, Cnc mill.
