====== Changing Rhino V6 licensing method ======

There are now three ways to use your Rhino V6 license. Your license can be utilized in any **one** the the following ways:
  * **Single-computer**, exclusively on a single computer
  * Floating on your network using the **LAN Zoo** [legacy]
  * Floating in the **Cloud Zoo** using the Internet [New]
[[https://www.rhino3d.com/6/new/licensing-and-administration|More license information]]

To change from one licensing method to another, you must remove your license from it's current use to make it available for use in another way.

==== Remove a Single-computer license ====

  - Start Rhino V6
  - From the **Tools** menu, click **Options**
  - Click the **Licenses** tab
  - Click the "**Change your license key**" button
  - Click the "**Remove License**" link

Your license is available to be used somewhere else.

> Note: You can also return a single-computer license validation by uninstalling Rhino V6.

==== Remove a Zoo license ====
  - Open the Zoo 6 Administrator on your Zoo server (Requires Administrator permissions)
  - Select the license to remove from the LAN Zoo
  - Click the red "X" in the toolbar icons

Your license is available to be used somewhere else.

==== Remove a Personal Cloud Zoo license ====
  - Go to the [[https://www.rhino3d.com|Rhino Web site]]
  - Click "**my account**"
  - Click **Licenses**
  - Click your name in the Personal licenses section
  - Click the license name
  - Click **Remove**


Your license is available to be used somewhere else.
