===== Rhino Display Troubleshooting =====

Many display problems can be solved by **[[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|updating your video drivers]]**. It is generally a good idea to keep drivers up to date as they can be updated quite often by the manufacturers.  

There may be other things you can do as well in particular cases. **Click the description below of the problem you are experiencing:**

  * **Was Windows 10 recently installed?** By default Windows 10 does not update drivers. Please follow **[[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|updating your video drivers]]** to solve any Windows 10 issues.
  * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:slow|Performance:]] Rotating the view or selecting objects is **slow**.
  * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|Commands fail]]: Pictures or decals don't show up or the image seen is **distorted**.
  * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:artifacts|Looks funny]]: There are strange **artifacts** on my screen.
  * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:missing_shadows|No shadows in Rendered Display Mode ]]: Comparing what I see on my computer to a previous computer or video, **no shadows**.
  * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|Crash]]: Rhino **crashes** and blames my **video card**.
  * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|Viewports strange order]]: Top viewport is the Front, and Front is Front...
  * [[rhino:5:video:Highres:Toolbars and text too small in 64-bit Rhino 5 on High Resolution screen]]


If none of these descriptions matches the problem you are experiencing, please email **tech@mcneel.com** with a detailed description of the problem as well as **[[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:failure|information about your video card]]**.
