=====사용자 지정한 Rhino 환경=====
여러분께서 직접 지정한 Rhino 환경 (작업 영역 파일) 을 공유하는 공간입니다.  만약 도구모음만 공유하시려면 [[ko:rhino:toolbarpage|도구모음 페이지]]를 이용해 주세요.

======Rhino 4 버전을 위한 사용자 지정 환경======

**[[http://en.wiki.mcneel.com/content/upload/files/MitchV4WIP.zip|Mitch가 지정한 4버전 작업 영역 WIP]]**
//<color red>0.7Mb 다운로드</color>//

{{http://en.wiki.mcneel.com/content/upload/images/MitchV4WS-Screenshot.jpg?499x364}}

늘 그러하듯이 작업은 계속 진행 중입니다. 자주 바뀝니다.  초기 1.1 버전용 작업 영역 배치에서 진화하여 이제는 4 버전 아이콘이 추가되었습니다.  I used V4's text button creation ability to create sets of menu-like toolbars for certain things. There are a few scripts under some of the buttons and lots of macros. A number of icons have been modified in an attempt to make them clearer (insofar as possible, V4 icons are just not "sharp").

This is designed to be used at a minimum of 1280 horizontally and 900 vertically.  For bigger displays, I have put in  some blank buttons as spacers, you will need to remove some or all of them to work at the minimum horizontal or vertical resolution.

본인의 책임 하에 사용하시기 바랍니다.  :)

======그 밖의 사용자 지정 작업 영역 (3 버전 등...)======

**[[http://en.wiki.mcneel.com/content/upload/files/gcwebsterRH3_SR4.zip|G. C. Webster's customized V3 Workspace]]**
//<color red>167Kb 다운로드</color>//

{{http://en.wiki.mcneel.com/content/upload/images/gcwebsterV3WS-Screenshot.jpg?421x365}}

it's possible to download my personal workspace file for Rhino 3.0, with custom buttons and organization. It contains regular commands, several macros, my own scripts and other useful codes from some VB masters such as [[http://www.avgauge.com|Dan Bayn]] , [[http://www.reconstructivism.net|David Rutten]] , [[http://www.morellosnc.it/m2/cad/rhino/|Emilio Morello]] and [[http://www.rayflectar-graphics.com/Rhino/RhinoScripts-Gallery.htm|Thomas Anagnostou.]]

It's composed basically by two big horizontal toolbars (and the osnap bar below) with flyouts in all buttons, leaving the most space possible to the graphics area. Almost all commands that we modellers often need (except some aliases) are available in these two toolbars, thanks to the flyouts. 이 작업 영역은 1280 x 1024 해상도를 위해 고안되었습니다.

최소한 Rhino 3.0 [[http://download.mcneel.com/whatsnew.asp?rel=51|서비스 릴리스 4]] 가 필요합니다.
