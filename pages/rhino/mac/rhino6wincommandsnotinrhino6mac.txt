======Rhino 6 for Windows commands not in Rhino 6 for Mac======

There are a number of Rhino 6 for Windows commands that are not yet in Rhino 6 for Mac.  Some commands are planned for addition in Rhino 6 for Mac, some have Mac-appropriate counterparts, and some are not on our roadmap.  Please post questions about specific missing commands in the [[http://discourse.mcneel.com/c/rhino-for-mac|Rhino for Mac Discourse category]] or check [[https://mcneel.myjetbrains.com/youtrack/issues?q=project:%20RH%20platform:%20mac%20%23unresolved|our issue tracker]] to see what is currently in development.
