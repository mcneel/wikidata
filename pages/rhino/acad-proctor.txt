======Academic Rhino Certification Exams======

====Qualifications====
An **Academic Rhino Proctor** (ARP) is:
  * A certified teacher in a state-accredited educational institution //(with an official school email)// with a valid **Rhinoceros Lab License**. 


The **ARP** administers the **[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/certified_test_academic|Rhino Academic Certification Exams]]** to students. 
  * The **ARP** certifies the results and testing environment for compliance with all testing site requirements and procedures.
  * The **ARP** unlocks academic tests for students and the proctor name is displayed on the student's certificate.
  * Download  [[http://s3.amazonaws.com/mcneel/rhino/5.0/training_docs/rhino_academic_testing_brochure_01.pdf|the Academic Rhino Certification Exams brochure.]]


==== How to purchase testing ====

You purchase testing in packs with full testing for 30 students. 10 tests are required per student.
  * **Up to 300 tests** = **full testing for 30 students**, includes **pretests, practice tests, and exams**.
  * **Option** to purchase multiple testing packs for larger classrooms.
  * Unused tests never expire. Roll them over to your next academic year.

The **Academic Rhino Testing** testing allows teachers to:
  * Add students to the testing system
  * Invite students to take the test by email
  * Monitor student progress and scoring.
  * Download student certificates

The **Academic Rhino Testing** gives students access to:
  * //Computerized tests with immediate scoring//
  * Test summary follow-up
  * Download PDFs of their certificates
  * History of their testing.

**Each student** will take:
  * **Practice Test** (10 questions) to test learning system
  * **Pretest of Level 1 Test** (40 questions) to measure initial knowledge at start of term
  * **Level 1 and Level 2 Exams** (contains three tests: //Part A, Part B, & Part C//)
  * Student gets a **personalized certificate**

  * Tests Part A, Part B and Part C can be offered over three class periods
  * Each test allows //two additional retakes// with in 90 days.
  * Students with passing scores may also retake twice for a better score
