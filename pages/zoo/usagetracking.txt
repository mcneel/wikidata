====== LAN Zoo Usage Tracking ======
> **Product:** //[[zoo:home|Zoo]]//
> **Summary:** //Enable usage tracking for LAN Zoo.//

===== Issue =====
I want to see who uses Rhino licenses by tracking license usage on LAN Zoo

===== Resolutions =====
  - Start **LAN Zoo Admin**
  - From the **Tools** menu, click **Options**
  - Select the **Enable license usage tracking** checkbox
