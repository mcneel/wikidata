======Support for Educators During Covid-19  ======
We know that schools are working remotely during this time and want to let you know we have support and resources available for you and your students.

  * [[https://discourse.mcneel.com/t/using-rhino-remotely-during-covid-19/98216|Using Rhino Remotely During COVID-19]] Forum Post
  * Free 90 day Rhino 6 Evaluation versions for [[https://www.rhino3d.com/download/rhino-for-windows/6/evaluation|Windows]] and [[https://www.rhino3d.com/download/rhino-for-mac/6/evaluation|Mac.]] The evaluation is fully functional for the first 90 days. After 90 days it is still functional but will not save.
  * [[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/6/trainingguides/en|Rhino 6 training manuals, videos and models]]
  * [[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/level1_test_academic|Rhino Academic Testing]] works remotely.
