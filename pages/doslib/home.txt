====== DOSLib ======
[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/doslib/home|{{:doslib:doslib.png|DOSLib}}]]

**LISP Library for CAD Applications**

DOSLib is a library of LISP-callable functions that provide functionality not available in CAD-based LISP programming languages, included with AutoCAD and BricsCAD.

DOSLib is open-source!

[[https://github.com/dalefugier/DOSLib|{{:doslib:github32.png|View on GitHub}}]]  **[[https://github.com/dalefugier/DOSLib|View on GitHub]]**

> **Important** //After 34 years, DOSLib is done - we are no longer maintaining this tool, and we will not be releasing new binaries to support new versions of AutoCAD and BricsCAD Pro.//

===== System requirements =====
DOSLib supports:
  * AutoCAD 2013 thru 2027
  * BricsCAD Pro V13 thru V26
