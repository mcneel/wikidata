====== Why does my Logitech mouse (cursor) slow down to a crawl inside Rhino? ======
> **Summary:** //How to configure a slow Logitech Mouse.//

=====Problem=====
Logitech drivers may assume that an OpenGl enabled application (like Rhino) is a game.

=====Solution=====
 - Open the mouse settings
 - Look for the Gaming settings.
 - Make sure Hardware Acceleration is not dis-abled for games.
