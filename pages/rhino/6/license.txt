======Rhino Licensing Options======

> **Product:** //[[rhino:home|Rhinoceros]]//
> **Summary:** //There are now **three** Rhino licensing options.//

There are three license methods: [[https://www.rhino3d.com/6/new/licensing-and-administration|Details...]]

  * The Cloud Zoo provides the maximum flexibility for individuals and teams. 
  * A private LAN Zoo works best for EDU Lab licenses. 

 **[[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/install/wizard/start|Choose the licensing method]]** that works best for you.
