On May 31 2007, Dutch Rhino Resellers organise in close co-operation with the industry a Rhino for Architects User meeting.

Partners/speakers are:

  * Pim Marsman, [[http://www.octatube.nl|Octatube]]
  * [[http://www.jlcoenders.nl|Jeroen Coenders]] , [[http://www.arup.com|Arup]] / [[http://www.structuraldesignlab.citg.tudelft.nl|Delft University]]
  * Sander Boer, Mecanoo
  * TU Delft, Faculty of [[rhino:architecture|Architecture]]
  * Nuno Almeida, UN Studio
  * Ramon van der Heijden, Eindhoven University
  * Pim van Wylick, de Architekten Cie.
  * Roderik van der Meulen, Benthem Crouwel

The focus will lie on experiences of users and the transfer of knowledge. There will also be plenty of time to meet other users.

* Computational design strategies informed by structural considerations
* Jeroen Coenders is a structural engineer at Arup involved in complex geometrical projects and a researcher at Delft University of Technology on novel computational strategies for structural design and engineering.
* He will present several issues in computation, structural design, software strategies and advanced tools related to the use of computational design strategies informed by structural considerations for geometrical (and structural) complex projects.
* These tools, resulting from the combination of theory and practice, have been built in several manners, amongst others as extensions of Rhino.

=====What's Pecha Kucha?=====
Born in Tokyo, a few years ago, Pecha Kucha is a unique show-and-tell event where creative people from all disciplines present their work, their ideas or even their holiday snaps.

Pecha Kucha roughly translates as chit-chat. Participants get to **show 20 slides for 20 seconds each**, meaning the audience experiences an exhilarating high speed journey through a kaleidoscope of inspirations, ideas and work.

<color white>.</color>

Bring 20 JPG's or a Power Point Presentation with you!
