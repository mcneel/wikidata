====Academic Rhino Proctor====

**Academic Rhino Proctor** (ARP) is a state certified teacher in an educational institution with a valid, official school email and a valid Rhino 5 Educational Lab License.  

The **ARP** will be allowed to administer the **Rhino Academic Certification Exams** to students. **ARP** will certify the results and testing environment for compliance with all testing site requirements and procedures. The Proctor will unlock academic tests for students and the proctor name will be displayed on the student's certificate.

To apply to become an **Academic Rhino Proctor**, please download [[http://s3.amazonaws.com/mcneel/rhino/5.0/training_docs/teacher_resources/RhinoProctorApplication2.pdf|this application]] and email or fax to [[mary@mcneel.com|Mary Fugier]] or fax to 206-545-7321.

Return to [[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/certified_test?&#authorizations|Certification and Authorizations Page.]]

===== How to Purchase =====
**Rhino Academic Tests are sold in 10 test bundles for $150.
**Each test pack includes the following:
  * Computerized tests with immediate scoring.
  * Test summary followup
  * Pretest of Level 1 Exam (Do at the beginning of the semester) (50 questions/ 1 hr.) 
  * One Level Final Exam contains three tests: Part A, Part B, & Part C
  * Each test contains 33 questions / test, 
  * 3 tests x 1 hr. per test = 3 hrs. total (test can be offered over 3 class periods.)
  * Passing Part A is required for Part B, Passing Part B is required for Part C. 
  * Passing Part C = Rhino Level 1 Certified Academic and student will get personalized certificate.
  * Each part Part A, Part B, Part C will allow for 2 additional retakes.
  * 2 Retakes are allowed for those with passing grades too. Students can retake for a better score.
  * A test pack is required //**for each student**// and can not be shared or transferred between two or more students.
