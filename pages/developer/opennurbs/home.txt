======openNURBS Toolkit======
[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/developer/opennurbs/home|{{:developer:opennurbs:opennurbs.png?128|openNURBS Toolkit}}]]


The openNURBS Initiative provides CAD, CAM, CAE, and computer graphics software developers the tools to accurately transfer 3-D geometry between applications. openNURBS is an open source toolkit for **only reading and writing** 3DM models. Our full-featured development platform is [[http://www.rhino3d.com|Rhino]].


For more information, support, developer guides and to download the C++ toolkit, .NET toolkit, or sample files, visit [[https://www.rhino3d.com/opennurbs|openNURBS]].
