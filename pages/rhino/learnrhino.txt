====== How to Learn Rhino======
===== Download and install the Rhino Evaluation.=====
If you have not already done this, download the [[http://download.rhino3d.com/rhino/5.0/evaluation/download/|Rhino 5 Evaluation]]

**Start with [[http://www.rhino3d.com/getting-started|Getting Started]].**

**Download the Rhino [[http://www.rhino3d.com/download/rhino/5.0/UsersGuide/|User's Guide and basic tutorials]].**

===== Rhino Level 1 and 2 Manuals =====

After doing the Getting Started tutorials, try working through the Rhino training guides.
  * [[http://www.rhino3d.com/download/rhino/5.0/Rhino5Level1Training|Rhino 5 Level 1 - PDF and Models (English).]] 
  * [[http://s3.amazonaws.com/mcneel/rhino/5.0/training_docs/Level 2/Rhino 5 Level2 PDF & Models.zip|Rhino 5 Level 2 - PDF and Models (English, Pre-release)]]

These manuals are designed to be use in Instructor Lead training, however may Rhino users feel they are helpful as self-paced training.
If you don't own Rhino, you can download them from [[http://www.rhino3d.com/download/rhino/5.0/evaluation|here.]]
