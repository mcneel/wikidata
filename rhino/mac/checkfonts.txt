===== Checking Your Fonts for Problems===== 

In **Applications**, start **Font Book**. 

In the **Collection** column, click **All Fonts** (at the top)

{{:rhino:mac:checkfonts1.png?nolink|}}

In the **Fonts** column, click a font.

Type **Command-A** to select all fonts.

{{:rhino:mac:checkfonts2.png|}}

On the **Application** menu, select **File > Validate Fonts** . A dialog appears and the program checks your fonts.

{{:rhino:mac:checkfonts3.png|}}

Fix any font problems you find.
