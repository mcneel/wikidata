====== Customer Story======
======Géry Gueville from Equation Models Kits ======

**[[http://cadcam.e-monsite.com/|Equation Models kits]]**

In the Rhino User Meeting Lyon 2009, Géry Gueville from [[http://cadcam.e-monsite.com/|Equation Models kits]] made an incredible presentation and showed us the entire process of how he manufactures aeroplane models, from the initial blueprints and surfaces to the final kit. He included profile generation, curvature analysis, assemblies, CNC milling, and visualization.
