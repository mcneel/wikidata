====== Using Rhino with Delcam PowerSHAPE ======

PowerSHAPE direct support for Rhino 3DM files: http://www.powershape-e.com/general/cad_data.asp

=====Rhino with PowerSHAPE=====
**PowerSHAPE** is a NURBS-based hybrid surface and solid modeler. It also has the ability to combine triangulated artwork, wrapped onto and trimmed into the model. Delcam calls this Total modeling.

A completely free version of **PowerSHAPE** (containing the full modeling functionality) is available: [[http://www.powershape-e.com|PowerSHAPE-e]].
