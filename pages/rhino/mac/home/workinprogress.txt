====== Rhino for Mac Work-In-Progress (WIP) ======

If you have purchased Rhino 5 for Mac, you have access to our [[http://www.rhino3d.com/go/download/rhino-for-mac/wip/latest|Work-In-Progress (WIP) build: RhinoWIP]].  These WIP builds are where we are testing out new code: new features, bug fixes, and other improvements.

[[http://www.rhino3d.com/go/download/rhino-for-mac/wip/latest|DOWNLOAD THE WIP]] (requires purchase of Rhino 5 for Mac)
__**RhinoWIP SHOULD NOT be used for mission-critical work as it may be unstable.**__

The [[http://www.rhino3d.com/go/download/rhino-for-mac/wip/latest|RhinoWIP]] build can be run side-by-side with the full, stable, commercial release...they are two separate applications:

{{ :rhino:mac:home:rhinowipindock.png?direct |}}



Rhinoceros (the commercial release) and RhinoWIP have two separate update systems.  RhinoWIP will be updated more frequently than the commercial release.

Rhinoceros and RhinoWIP share preferences and a license.
