===== Adding a license key in use in use in the Zoo or in a standalone device to your Rhino account =====

In order to add a license in use to your Rhino account, you must remove it from the Zoo or the device it's being used on first.

==== Removing the license from its existing location ====

== If the license is on the Zoo: ==
  - Remove the license from the Zoo.

== If the license is on a standalone device: ==
  - Go to **Tools** -> **Options** -> **Licenses** -> **Change License Key**
  - Click **Remove License** at the bottom of the dialog.

==== Adding the license to Rhino accounts ====
  - See [[rhino_accounts:add_licenses|Adding a License]].
