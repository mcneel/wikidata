===== Rhino Account/CloudZoo Reporting Feature =====

The **RhinoAccounts/CloudZoo** Team has team a very detailed CSV reporting feature.

To access reporting, do the following: 

  - Go to your **Rhino account** [[https://www.rhino3d.com/my-account/|here]]
  - Pick **Cloud Zoo Teams** and **Licenses**-> Select your Personal account or Team.
  - Pick on the **Rhino license** in the account.
  - Then pick on **View Historical Usage** button
