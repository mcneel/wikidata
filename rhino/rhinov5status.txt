====== Rhino 5.0 Status ======

Rhino 5.0 is in Beta.  If you own a copy of Rhino 4.0, then you may download the Rhino 5.0 Beta [[http://download.rhino3d.com/rhino/5.0/beta|here]].  A new build of the Rhino 5.0 Beta is generally published every Wednesday.  When it is available, and your old Rhino 5.0 Beta will ask you if you want to update to the newer version.
