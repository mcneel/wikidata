====== Can I Use PanelingTools? (Rhino for Mac) ======
> **Summary:** //Does the plug-in PanelingTools exist for Rhino for Mac?//

Yes. PanelingTools plug-in is part of the released Rhino for Mac. The PanelingTools menu and the commands are available through the command line only.  Type **pt** to see all the PanelingTools commands in the AutoComplete list.
