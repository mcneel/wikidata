======Video Troubleshooting: What Rhino Sees======

To help diagnose this problem, determine what brand of video card Rhino sees in your system:
  - **Start Rhino 5**
  - From the **Tools** menu, click **Options**
  - Expand the **View** tab, then click **OpenGL**
  - In the **Video Hardware & Driver Information** section, read the brand of your video card:
  - Select your video card manufacturer:
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:intel|Intel]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:nvidia|NVidia]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:amd|AMD]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:microsoft|Microsoft GDI Generic]]
    * [[rhino:5:video:troubleshooting:virtual|VMware, Galium, Parallels]]
