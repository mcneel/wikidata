====== CoffeeBreak ======
> **Summary:** //CoffeeBreak plugin for Rhino4//

\\

=====Purpose=====

The purpose of this plugin is to make your boss think you're taking a **necessary** coffee-break, because your computer is bogged down calculating something very complicated.

\\

{{http://en.wiki.mcneel.com/content/upload/images/CoffeeBreakDialog.png}}

\\

Run the command _CoffeeBreak and a dialog with a never ending progression of nonsense data will be displayed on the screen. By downloading this plugin you agree to the terms and conditions as specified by the distributor. Essentially, the terms state that you are not allowed to use this plugin if you drink decaf.
