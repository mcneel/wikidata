====== Additional Hatch Patterns to Download ======

====Hatch patterns for Rhino 5====

1. Download [[http://wiki.mcneel.com/_media/legacy/en/hatchpatterns_1033.zip|this ZIP file]] and extract the contents. Rename hatchpatterns.txt to hatchpatterns.pat. 

2. In Rhino 5, go to File>Properties>Annotation>Hatch and simply import hatchpatterns.pat


===== Hatch patterns for Rhino 4 =====
===Hatch patterns by Carlotto Vittorio===

Just download this text file and replace the original in (We suggest you save a copy of the original):

  * Win XP: C:\Documents and Settings\All Users\Application Data\McNeel\Rhinoceros\4.0\English\Support
  * Vista & Win7: C:\ProgramData\McNeel\Rhinoceros\4.0\English\Support
