===== Add a License to your Rhino Account =====

  - Check your inbox for an email from us or a reseller containing licensing information.
  - If the email has a link titled Install your license key:
    * Click on the link.
    * Once the link opens, click on Rhino Account.
  - If the email does not have a link titled Install your license key:
   * Find the license key for the product in the email.
   * Copy the license key.
   * Go to the Add License page.
  - Add the license key to your personal account or one of the teams you belong to, if any.
