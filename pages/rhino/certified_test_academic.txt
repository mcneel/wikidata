====Rhino Certified Academic Testing====
Return to [[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/certified_test?&#authorizations|Certification and Authorizations Page.]]

**Rhino Academic Testing is sold in bundles. There are 10 test packs per Bundle for $150.**

Each test pack includes the following:
  * Computerized tests with immediate scoring.
  * Test summary followup
  * Pretest of Level 1 Exam (Do at the beginning of the semester) (50 questions/ 1 hr.) 
  * One Level Final Exam contains three tests: A, B, & C
  * Each test contains 33 questions / test, 
  * 3 tests x 1 hr. per test = 3 hrs. total (test can be offered over 3 class periods.)
  * Passing A is required for B, Passing B is required for C. 
  * Passing C = Rhino Level 1 Certified Academic and student will get personalized certificate.
  * Each part A, B, C will allow for 2 additional retakes.
  * 2 Retakes are allowed for those with passing grades too. Students can retake for a better score.
  * A test pack is required for each student and can not be shared or transferred between two or more students.

To get started:
  - Fill out ARP Academic Rhino Proctor application and send it to me.
  - When you are approved, you can buy the Rhino Academic Testing Bundle.
  - Then you will create a login on the testing system. We will give you rights so you can then add students to the system and schedule them to  take the test. 
  - Please call or email Jody when you have used your 10 testing packs. She will be happy to get you more.
