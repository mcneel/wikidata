====== Brazil for Rhino======



|{{:legacy:en:SplutterFishGlassLogo.png}} {{:legacy:en:RhinoGlassLogo.png}}|


===== Notes =====
This plug-in is now a shipped product.  Check the [[http://brazil.mcneel.com//|Brazil for Rhino website]] for details.

A great rendering engine, although it is not the only great rendering engine available. Look at the gallery on the Brazil [[http://brazil.rhino3d.com/forum|forum]] to see users' posted images to see the wide variety of images possible in Brazil. There is also a link for the materials you may want to download.

Tightly integrated with Rhino, but not easy to master. There is quite a learning curve to get the material //look// you are after. 

See the [[http://brazil.rhino3d.com/profiles/blogs/brazil-rs-feature-list|feature list]].

[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/render_differences|Differences between Brazil, Flamingo, and Penguin.]]
