====Rhino Authorized Academic Proctor====

**Rhino Authorized Academic Proctor**, RAAP is a state certified teacher in an educational institution with valid, official school email and a valid Rhino 5 Educational Lab License.  

The **RAAP** will be allowed to administer the **Rhino Academic Certification Exams** to students. **RAAP** will certify the results and testing environment for compliance with all testing site requirements and procedures. Proctor will unlock academic tests for students and the proctor name will be display on the student's certificate.

To apply to become an **Rhino Authorized Academic Proctor**, please fill out the following application and email or fax to Mary Fugier mary@mcneel.com 206-545-7321.
