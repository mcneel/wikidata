======openNURBS Toolkit======
[[http://wiki.mcneel.com/developer/opennurbs/home|{{:developer:opennurbs:opennurbs.png?128|openNURBS Toolkit}}]]


=====Overview=====
The openNURBS Initiative provides CAD, CAM, CAE, and computer graphics software developers the tools to accurately transfer 3-D geometry between applications.

The tools provided by openNURBS include:

  * [[http://download.rhino3d.com/openNURBS/5.0/release/download/|openNURBS 5 C++ source SDK]] for Windows, Mac, iOS, Android and Linux.
  * [[https://github.com/mcneel/rhinocommon/wiki/Rhino3dmIO-Toolkit-(OpenNURBS-build)|openNURBS 5 .NET SDK]]
  * [[http://download.rhino3d.com/openNURBS/5.0/opennurbs5samples|Sample 3D files]]
  * [[http://wiki.mcneel.com/developer/on5migrate|openNURBS 5 Migration Guide]]
  * [[http://discourse.mcneel.com/category/opennurbs | Technical support forum]] 
  * [[developer:opennurbs:faq|FAQ]]
  * [[developer:opennurbs:opennurbs_5_20130711_release_notes|Latest release notes]]


openNURBS is an open source toolkit for **only reading and writing** 3DM models. Our full-featured development platform is [[http://www.rhino3d.com|Rhino]].
