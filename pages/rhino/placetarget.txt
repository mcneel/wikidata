====== Reassign Camera Target Point======

>Reassigning a camera target point is handy when you run into your target while zooming 



=====To start the command=====

  * Click the {{:legacy:en:target.gif}} button located on the Set View toolbar
  * You can also assign the following to your own button or a command in the alias list: 
! //-ViewportProperties //CameraTarget //Enter //Pause !

=====Tip=====
For full control of your camera use the **Camera** command (keyboard shortcut **F6**).

This will Show/Hide the Camera-cone with draggable control points for all camera properties.
