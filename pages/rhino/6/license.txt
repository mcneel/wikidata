======Rhino and plug-in Licensing Options======

> **Product:** //[[rhino:home|Rhinoceros, and selected plug-ins]]//
> **Summary:** //There are now **three** Rhino licensing options.//

There are three license methods: [[https://www.rhino3d.com/6/new/licensing-and-administration|Details...]]

  * The **Cloud Zoo** floating license manager provides the maximum flexibility for individuals and teams. 
  * The **LAN Zoo** floating license manager works best for EDU Lab licenses. 

 **[[https://wiki.mcneel.com/rhino/install/wizard/start|Choose the licensing method]]** that works best for you.
